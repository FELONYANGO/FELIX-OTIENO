Kotieno Primary School Kokech, Kenya

Kotieno Primary School is a public primary school in Kokech. This school is run by religious organization. It is a day school. This educational institution is a integrated and mixed type of institution.The pupil to classroom ratio in this school is 55.5:1 and the pupil to toilet ratio is 44.4:1. There are total 8 classrooms, 5 boys toilets, 5 girls toilets and 3 teachers toilets. The total numbers of students enrolls in this school are 444. GOK TSC (Teachers Service Commission) males are 12 There are 1 non-teaching staff males and 1 female non-teaching staff in Kotieno Primary School, Kokech.
